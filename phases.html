Phases

  1. Pre-Game- Before the game begins, choosing the team composition is extremely important and requires a lot of strategy.
  2. Laning Phase- The phase in which summoners simply farm, or train their champions to become stronger.
  3. Ganking Phase- The phase in which certain summoners come into other summoner's lanes to give their allies an advantage against their opponents.
  4. Teamfighting Phase- The phase in which all the champions on one team fight all the champions on the opposing team.
